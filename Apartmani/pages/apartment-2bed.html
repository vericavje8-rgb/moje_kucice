About Apartman Alvin

Discover comfort in this spacious 2-bedroom coastal apartment located directly by the sea near Novigrad's historic old town center. This first-floor retreat is perfect for families or groups seeking authentic Croatian hospitality with modern amenities.

The apartment features two comfortable bedrooms each with double beds (1.6x2m), a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, and a modern bathroom with washing machine. Guests enjoy access to a shared terrace where each apartment has its own private corner with sea views. The apartment includes satellite TV, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and all essential amenities for a perfect Adriatic getaway.
