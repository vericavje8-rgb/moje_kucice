About Apartman App1 - Libera

Experience authentic Croatian coastal living in this charming 1-bedroom apartment located directly by the sea, just steps from Novigrad's historic old town center. This intimate seaside retreat offers everything needed for a perfect Adriatic getaway.

Positioned on the first floor with immediate proximity to the sea, this cozy apartment features one comfortable bedroom, a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, and a modern bathroom. The shared terrace provides each apartment with its own private corner - perfect for morning coffee or evening relaxation while enjoying stunning sea views and listening to the gentle sounds of the Adriatic waves.

Located right on the waterfront with direct beach access, you can step out and be on the beach within moments. The historic charm of Novigrad's old town is just a pleasant stroll away, offering authentic Croatian dining, local markets, and cultural experiences.
