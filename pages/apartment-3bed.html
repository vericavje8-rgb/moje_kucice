About Tomas Villa

Experience the ultimate family comfort in this spacious villa-style apartment located directly by the sea near Novigrad's historic old town center. This ground-floor villa features three bedrooms plus a separate living room, making it perfect for larger families or groups seeking a traditional Croatian experience.

The villa includes three comfortable bedrooms each with double beds, a separate living room with sofa, a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, and modern bathroom facilities. Guests enjoy access to a shared terrace where the villa has its own private corner with sea views. Premium amenities include satellite TV, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, washing machine, and a full range of kitchen appliances including oven, microwave, coffee machine, and toaster.

Spanning 100 square meters of thoughtfully designed space, this traditional villa offers an authentic Croatian living experience with modern comforts. Every detail has been carefully maintained to provide comfort and elegance while preserving the villa's authentic character for family-oriented guests.
