About Apartman Tomas

Experience the ultimate comfort in this spacious villa-style apartment located directly by the sea near Novigrad's historic old town center. This first-floor apartment features two bedrooms plus a separate living room, making it perfect for larger families or groups.

The apartment includes two comfortable bedrooms each with double beds (1.6x2m), a separate living room with sofa, a fully equipped kitchen with dining area, and two modern bathrooms. Guests enjoy access to a shared terrace where each apartment has its own private corner with sea views. Premium amenities include HDTV, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, washing machine, and a full range of kitchen appliances including oven, microwave, coffee machine, and toaster.

Spanning 1,200 square feet of meticulously designed space, this penthouse offers an unparalleled living experience with its gourmet kitchen, spacious bedrooms, and private terrace. Every detail has been carefully curated to provide the ultimate in comfort and elegance for the most discerning guests.
